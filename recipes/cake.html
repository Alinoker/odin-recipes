Whole Grain Bread

>

Description

My favorite bread, I always do this one.

Ingredients:

  • 200g white flour (plain)
  • 300g coarse brown flour (whole wheat)
  • 3 tablespoons bran
  • 2 tablespoons wheat germ
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon treacle (molasses)
  • 600 ml milk (half milk, half water)
  • 70g seeds (sunflower, pumpkin, chia, cashews, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C for 20 minutes.
  2. Bake at 200°C for 20 minutes.
  3. Reduce the temperature to 170°C and bake for an additional hour.
