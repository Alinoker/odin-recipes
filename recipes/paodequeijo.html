Pao de Queijo

Description

Brazilian bread made from and for the Gods.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 egg whites
  • 6 slices of mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of chemical baking powder
  • Grated Parmesan

Instructions:

  1. Beat the eggs, egg whites and cheese in a blender
  2. add the yeast last
  3. Distribute the dough into molds
  4. Add the grated parmesan on top
  5. Place in the preheated oven at 180Â°C for 15 minutes. Yield: 6 servings
